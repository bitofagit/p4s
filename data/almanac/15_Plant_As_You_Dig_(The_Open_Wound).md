# Plant As You Dig (The Open Wound)

In nature, bare soil is an emergency. If the earth is exposed, it will rapidly
scab over with aggressive pioneer weeds to protect itself from the sun.

## The Cost of Exposure

When you clear wild meadow, you create a bare cultivated tile. This exposed dirt
suffers **Flash Evaporation**, losing moisture much faster than wild grass.

> **The Open Wound:** Bare dirt acts as a wound. Every tile of unplanted,
> unmulched soil aggressively bleeds vitality.

## Strategy

Never dig more than you can immediately cover. Prioritise planting or mulching
on the same week you disturb the soil.
