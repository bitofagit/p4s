# SG_YORK_08: The Comfrey Engine

The most efficient mineral‑cycling guild in the Yorkshire roster.

### Requirements:

- 1× **Dynamic Accumulator** (e.g., Comfrey)
- 1× **Mulch Producer**
- 1× **Heavy Feeder**

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.0 (utility guild)
- **Bonus:** +1.5 mineral fixation to the core plant

### Explanation:

A single Comfrey plant adjacent to a heavy feeder activates a closed mineral
loop. The taproot mines subsoil minerals and deposits them as mulch on the
surface—directly where shallow feeder roots can absorb them.
