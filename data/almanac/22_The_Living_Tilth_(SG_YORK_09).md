# SG_YORK_09: The Living Tilth

Used to prepare compacted clay soil for delicate root vegetables.

### Requirements:

- 1× **Groundcover** (e.g., White Clover)
- 1× **Nitrogen Fixer**
- 1× **Root Crop** (e.g., Daikon)

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.3
- **Bonus:** high nitrogen support and compaction relief

### Explanation:

Clover carpets the floor, locking in moisture and nitrogen, while a root crop
below physically opens channels through compacted soil.
