# SG_YORK_04: The Hungry Valley

Designed for heavy‑feeding brassicas and northern perennials.

### Requirements:

- 2× **Leafy Crops** (e.g., Cabbage, Rhubarb)
- 1× **Nitrogen Fixer** (e.g., Broad Bean)

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.5
- **Bonus:** stabilises nitrogen levels under intensive feeding

### Explanation:

Rhubarb and cabbage are famously hungry crops that exhaust even good loam within
a few seasons. When a nitrogen fixer stands between them—locking nitrogen into
the topsoil turn by turn—they reach maximum yield without crashing the soil
chemistry.
