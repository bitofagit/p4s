# The Weekly Cycle

A permaculture system is not built in a day. It requires patient observation and
deliberate interaction. Your farm runs on a weekly turn cycle.

## Player Resources

- **Energy**: Physical stamina. Clearing land and planting crops consumes Energy.
  It resets every week.
- **Actions**: Logistical bandwidth. Moving infrastructure or building swales
  costs Actions.
- **Capacity (£)**: Your overall farm capital. Used to buy seeds and
  infrastructure.

## The Golden Rule

Do not just click “Next Week” to speed up time. Observe water levels, check
soil nitrogen, and ensure your system is stable before advancing.
