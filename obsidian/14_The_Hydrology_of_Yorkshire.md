# Hydrology & Swales

Water is the lifeblood of the Pennines, but it is often too much or too little at the wrong time. 

### The Swale System
Swales are level-bottomed trenches designed to stop water in its tracks. Instead of water running off the clay and into the stream, a swale forces it to "Stop, Spread, and Soak."

- **Storage:** Swales hold moisture for the surrounding 3x3 area.
- **Diffusion:** Moisture from a swale will slowly migrate into adjacent tiles over several turns.
- **Evaporation:** Uncovered swales lose water quickly. Surround them with [[12_Understanding_Guilds]] to create a protective canopy.
