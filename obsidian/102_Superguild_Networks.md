# Superguild Networks

While standard guilds work **vertically** on a single tile, **Superguilds** work
**horizontally** across a **3×3 neighbourhood**.

## Role‑Based Synergy (3×3)

Superguilds do not care about specific plant names; they care about **ecological
roles**. If the required number of roles are present within the 9‑tile grid
surrounding a center point, a superguild is formed.

### Common Roles

- **Nitrogen Fixers**: Restore soil nitrogen.
- **Dynamic Accumulators**: Mine deep minerals.
- **Windbreaks**: Reduce desiccation / evaporation stress.

## Visual Cue (Guild Vision)

Active superguild networks are displayed as a **Glowing Purple Web** connecting
the contributing tiles.

## Reading Requirements

Each superguild page lists:

- **### Requirements:** the exact role counts needed within 3×3
- **### Bonuses:** yield multiplier and any special effects
- **### Explanation:** why the ecology works (and the Yorkshire context when relevant)
