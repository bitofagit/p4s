# SG_YORK_11: The Clay Breakers

A specialised superguild designed specifically for the heavy, compacted soils of
the Yorkshire Pennines.

### Requirements:

- 2× **Root Crops**
- 2× **Structural Plants**

### Bonuses:

- **Effect:** shatters compacted clay from multiple depths simultaneously.
- **Legacy Effect:** fractured clay releases bound minerals and becomes workable
  loam, permanently improving the mineral baseline of the neighbourhood.

### Explanation:

Daikon drives vertically while potato heaves the topsoil laterally. Multiple
depths of disturbance open channels for water, roots, and soil life.
