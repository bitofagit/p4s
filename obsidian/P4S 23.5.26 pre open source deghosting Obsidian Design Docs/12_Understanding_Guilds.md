# Understanding Guilds

A **Standard Guild** is the foundational unit of our ecosystem. It operates on a **1-Tile Vertical Principle**.

### Vertical Stacking
To trigger a standard guild, you must occupy the three vertical layers of a single tile:
1. **Canopy** (Trees)
2. **Understory** (Shrubs/Large Veg)
3. **Ground** (Leafy crops/Groundcovers)

When a "Core" plant (like an Apple Tree) is placed on the same tile as its required "Companions," the growth rate is doubled and the harvest yield is significantly increased.

**Visual Cue:** In Guild Vision, satisfied 1-tile guilds will glow with a **Neon Gold Aura**.
