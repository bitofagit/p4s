# SG_YORK_05: The Forest Floor Harvest

A guild for the high-value understory shrubs that thrive in the Yorkshire damp.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 1x **Fruit Shrub** (e.g., Blackcurrant)
- 1x **Groundcover** (e.g., Alpine Strawberry)
- 2x **Shade Tolerant** species

**Yield Multiplier:** x1.8
**Bonus:** High moisture retention and shade exploitation.

**Explanation:**
In the deep shadow of a canopy, these species unlock yields that would be impossible in open sun. The groundcover locks moisture into the shaded soil, creating a self-sustaining understory.
