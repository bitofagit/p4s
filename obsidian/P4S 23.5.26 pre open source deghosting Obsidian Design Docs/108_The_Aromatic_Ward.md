# SG_YORK_07: The Aromatic Ward

A defensive guild used to shield heavy feeders from airborne pests.

### Requirements:

- 2× **Pest Repellents**
- 2× **Aromatic Plants** (e.g., Rosemary, Mint)

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.4
- **Bonus:** significant reduction in nitrogen drain for all neighbours

### Explanation:

Volatile aromatic compounds overlap and amplify each other’s pest‑confusing
effects. Flying insects cannot locate target crops through the aromatic fog,
allowing heavy feeders in the 3×3 area to grow undisturbed.
