# SG_YORK_08: The Comfrey Engine

The most efficient mineral-cycling guild in the Yorkshire roster.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 1x **Dynamic Accumulator** (e.g., Comfrey)
- 1x **Mulch Producer**
- 1x **Heavy Feeder**

**Yield Multiplier:** x1.0 (Utility Guild)
**Bonus:** +1.5 Mineral Fixation (M) to the core plant.

**Explanation:**
A single Comfrey plant adjacent to a heavy feeder activates a closed mineral loop. The taproot mines subsoil minerals and deposits them as mulch on the surface—directly where the heavy feeder's shallow roots can absorb them.
