# SG_YORK_07: The Aromatic Ward

A defensive guild used to shield heavy feeders from airborne pests.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 2x **Pest Repellents**
- 2x **Aromatic Plants** (e.g., Rosemary, Mint)

**Yield Multiplier:** x1.4
**Bonus:** Significant reduction in Nitrogen drain for all neighbours.

**Explanation:**
Volatile aromatic compounds overlap and amplify each other's pest-confusing effects. Flying insects cannot locate target crops through the aromatic fog, allowing the heavy feeders in the 3x3 area to grow undisturbed.
