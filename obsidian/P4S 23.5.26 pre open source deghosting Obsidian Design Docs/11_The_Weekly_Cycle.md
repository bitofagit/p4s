# The Weekly Cycle

A permaculture system is not built in a day. It requires patient observation and deliberate interaction. Your farm runs on a weekly turn cycle.

### Player Resources
- **Energy:** Represents your physical stamina. Clearing land and planting crops consumes Energy. It resets every week.
- **Actions:** Represents your logistical bandwidth. Moving infrastructure or building swales takes Actions.
- **Capacity (£):** Your overall farm capital. Used to buy seeds and infrastructure.

**The Golden Rule:** Do not just click 'Next Week' to speed up time. Observe the changing water levels, check your soil nitrogen, and ensure your system is stable before advancing.
