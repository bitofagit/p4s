# UI: The Soil Profile Cross-Section
**Tags:** #ui #ux #inspector

When the player opens the Deep Inspector, we replace the standard tooltip with a visual "Slice of Cake" representing the exact state of the 9 ecosystem metrics.

## Visual Design Mapping
* **Depth:** Physically dictates the vertical height of the brown dirt on the screen before hitting the grey rock layer.
* **Structure:** Alters the drawn texture of the dirt (hard, chunky clay blocks vs. fluffy, aerated soil crumbs).
* **Moisture:** A translucent blue water-table line rising or falling through the dirt slice.
* **Fungi:** Glowing white, web-like mycelial veins branching through the cross-section.
* **Bacteria:** A warm, golden, shimmering bioluminescence overlay in the dirt.
* **Macro-Life:** Animated worms and beetles wiggling inside the soil bounds.
* **pH & Toxicity:** Displayed via a pinned Litmus Test strip UI and a dynamic Hazard icon on the side of the slice.
