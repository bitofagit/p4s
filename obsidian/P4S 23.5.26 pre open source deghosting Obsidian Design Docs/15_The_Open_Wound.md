# Plant As You Dig

In nature, bare soil is an emergency. If the earth is exposed, it will rapidly scab over with aggressive pioneer weeds to protect itself from the sun. 

### The Cost of Exposure
When you use the Spade to clear wild meadow, you create a bare, cultivated tile. This exposed dirt suffers from **Flash Evaporation**, losing moisture three times faster than wild grass.

> [!WARNING] 
> **The Open Wound:** Bare dirt acts as a wound. Every tile of unplanted, unmulched soil aggressively bleeds **Vitality**. 

**The Strategy:** Never dig more than you can immediately cover. Prioritise planting seeds or laying down woodchip on the exact same week you turn the soil.
