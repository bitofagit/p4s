# The Physical Layer (The Earth)
**Tags:** #mechanics #physical #soil

These are the structural properties of the soil. They dictate what can physically fit in the ground and how well it drains.

## Abstracted UI Stats
* **Structure (0-10):** How loose or compacted the soil is.
  * *High (10):* Fluffy crumbs. Easy root penetration, excellent drainage.
  * *Low (0):* Chunky rocks / hardpan clay. Stunted roots, prone to waterlogging.
* **Moisture (0-10):** The current water content.
  * *Dynamics:* Drains faster if Structure is high. Evaporates faster if there is no Canopy cover.

## Real-World UI Stats (Not Abstracted)
* **Topsoil Depth (0cm - 60cm+):** How deep the brown dirt goes before hitting grey bedrock.
  * *Low (0cm):* Scraped earth. Only shallow weeds survive.
  * *High (60cm+):* Required for massive taproots (Oak trees) or deep root veg (Daikon).
* **Soil Temperature (°C):** The actual ground warmth.
  * *Mechanic:* Seed germination is bottlenecked by soil temperature, not air temperature.
* **Sunlight Exposure (Hours per Day):** How much light hits the tile.
  * *Scale:* 0 Hours (Deep Shade) to 8+ Hours (Full Sun).
