# SG_YORK_01: The Boggart's Brake

A survival guild for the waterlogged valleys and wind-swept ridges of the Pennines.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 1x **Nitrogen Fixer** (e.g., Alder)
- 1x **Wetland Drainer** (e.g., Willow/Reed)
- 1x **Windbreak** (e.g., Hawthorn)

**Yield Multiplier:** x1.0 (Utility Guild)
**Bonus:** Massive boost to N-Fixation and Evaporation Resistance.

**Explanation:**
Alder's Frankia root bacteria explode into activity when a windbreak shelters the boggy ground from desiccating winds. The flooded clay becomes a nitrogen engine, fixing N at a rate impossible in dry soil.
