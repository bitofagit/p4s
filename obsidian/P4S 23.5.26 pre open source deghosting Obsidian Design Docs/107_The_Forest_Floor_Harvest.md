# SG_YORK_05: The Forest Floor Harvest

A guild for high‑value understory shrubs that thrive in the Yorkshire damp.

### Requirements:

- 1× **Fruit Shrub** (e.g., Blackcurrant)
- 1× **Groundcover** (e.g., Alpine Strawberry)
- 2× **Shade Tolerant** species

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.8
- **Bonus:** high moisture retention and shade exploitation

### Explanation:

In the deep shadow of a canopy, these species unlock yields that would be
impossible in open sun. The groundcover locks moisture into the shaded soil,
creating a self‑sustaining understory.
