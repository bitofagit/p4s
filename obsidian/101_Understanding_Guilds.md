# Understanding Guilds

A **Standard Guild** is the foundational unit of our ecosystem. It operates on
a **1‑Tile Vertical Principle**.

## Vertical Stacking (1 Tile)

To trigger a standard guild, you must occupy the three vertical layers of a
single tile:

1. **Canopy** (trees)
2. **Understory** (shrubs / large veg)
3. **Ground** (leafy crops / groundcovers)

When a “Core” plant (like an Apple Tree) is placed on the same tile as its
required “Companions”, growth and harvest output are boosted.

## Visual Cue (Guild Vision)

- **Satisfied 1‑tile guilds** glow with a **Neon Gold Aura**.
- Superguilds are separate: see [[13_Superguild_Networks]].
