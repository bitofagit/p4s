# SG002: Orchard Core Guild

This is the classic permaculture trio designed for long‑term food security.

### Requirements:

- 1× **Fruit Tree** (the core)
- 1× **Dynamic Accumulator** (the miner)
- 1× **Nitrogen Fixer** (the fertiliser)

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.8
- **Mineral Stability:** significantly slows mineral decay

### Explanation:

The nitrogen fixer restores what the fruit tree plunders, while the dynamic
accumulator (like Comfrey) mines deep minerals and deposits them as surface
mulch.
