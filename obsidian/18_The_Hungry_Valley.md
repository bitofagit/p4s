# SG_YORK_04: The Hungry Valley

Designed for heavy-feeding brassicas and northern perennials.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 2x **Leafy Crops** (e.g., Cabbage, Rhubarb)
- 1x **Nitrogen Fixer** (e.g., Broad Bean)

**Yield Multiplier:** x1.5
**Bonus:** Stabilizes Nitrogen levels under intensive feeding.

**Explanation:**
Rhubarb and Cabbage are famously hungry crops that exhaust even good loam within a few seasons. When a nitrogen fixer stands between them—locking N into the topsoil turn by turn—they reach maximum yield without crashing the soil chemistry.
