# SG002: Orchard Core Guild

This is the classic permaculture trio designed for long-term food security.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 1x **Fruit Tree** (The Core)
- 1x **Dynamic Accumulator** (The Miner)
- 1x **Nitrogen Fixer** (The Fertilizer)

**Bonuses:**
- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.8
- **Mineral Stability:** Significantly slows M-decay.

**Explanation:**
The Nitrogen Fixer restores what the Fruit Tree plunders, while the Dynamic Accumulator (like Comfrey) mines deep minerals and deposits them as surface mulch.
