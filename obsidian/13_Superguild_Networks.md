# Superguild Networks

While standard guilds work vertically, **Superguilds** work horizontally across a **3x3 Neighbourhood**.

### Role-Based Synergy
Superguilds do not care about specific plant names; they care about **Ecological Roles**. If the required number of roles are present within the 9-tile grid surrounding a center point, a Superguild is formed.

**Common Roles:**
- **Nitrogen Fixers:** Restore soil N-levels.
- **Dynamic Accumulators:** Mine deep minerals.
- **Pollinators:** Increase yield for flowering neighbours.

**Visual Cue:** In Guild Vision, these networks are displayed as a **Glowing Purple Web** connecting the contributors.
