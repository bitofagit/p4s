# Soil Ecosystem (MOC)
**Tags:** #game-design #mechanics #p4s #permaculture

## Design Philosophy
The soil in Permaculture 4 Squares is not a static container; it is an active, shifting ecosystem. We simulate this through a 9-stat system, categorised into three distinct layers.

To avoid spreadsheet fatigue, players interact with these stats via high-level visual UI abstractions, primarily the [[04_UI_Soil_Profile]].

## The Three Layers
* **[[01_Physical_Layer]]:** The structural earth (Structure, Depth, Moisture, Temp, Sun).
* **[[02_Chemical_Layer]]:** The raw fuel and hazards (Fertility, pH, Toxicity).
* **[[03_Biological_Layer]]:** The living engines of the dirt (Fungi, Bacteria, Macro-Life).
