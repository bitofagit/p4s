# SG_YORK_01: The Boggart’s Brake

A survival guild for the waterlogged valleys and wind‑swept ridges of the Pennines.

### Requirements:

- 1× **Nitrogen Fixer** (e.g., Alder)
- 1× **Wetland Drainer** (e.g., Willow / Reed)
- 1× **Windbreak** (e.g., Hawthorn)

### Bonuses:

- **Yield Multiplier:** x1.0 (utility guild)
- **Bonus:** massive boost to nitrogen fixation and evaporation resistance

### Explanation:

Alder’s Frankia root bacteria explode into activity when a windbreak shelters the
boggy ground from desiccating winds. The flooded clay becomes a nitrogen engine,
fixing N at a rate impossible in dry soil.
