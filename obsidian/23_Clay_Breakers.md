# SG_YORK_11: The Clay Breakers

A specialized Superguild designed specifically for the heavy, compacted soils of the Yorkshire Pennines.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 2x **Root Crops**
- 2x **Structural Plants**

**Effect:**
Shatters compacted clay from multiple depths simultaneously. Daikon drives vertically while Potato heaves the topsoil laterally. 

**Legacy Effect:**
The fractured clay releases bound minerals and becomes workable loam, permanently improving the Mineral baseline of the neighborhood.
