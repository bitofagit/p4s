# SG_YORK_09: The Living Tilth

Used to prepare compacted clay soil for delicate root vegetables.

**Requirements (3x3 Area):**
- 1x **Groundcover** (e.g., White Clover)
- 1x **Nitrogen Fixer**
- 1x **Root Crop** (e.g., Daikon)

**Yield Multiplier:** x1.3
**Bonus:** High Nitrogen bonus and compaction relief.

**Explanation:**
Clover carpets the floor, locking in moisture and nitrogen, while a root crop below physically opens channels through compacted soil. This creates ideal growing conditions for delicate crops like Carrots that would otherwise fork and fail in the clay.
