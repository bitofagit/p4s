P4S opencore — pixel art drop zones (res://assets/base/sprites/)
Premium fork mirrors this tree under res://assets/premium/sprites/ (git-ignored).

Convention: PNG, nearest-neighbour / pixel snap. Plants: 128×128 → atlas stitch OR user://databases/sprites/{id}.png
Map structures: match data/data_objects.gd atlas_x when used on StructureLayer tile atlas.

── ui/icons/tools ──
Rotovate, scythe, uproot, harvest, chop_and_drop, dig_swale, build_mound, water_tile, hosepipe,
apply_tea, e_tiller, plant/seed, build, build_path, demolish, additive (compost/mulch), empty hand.

── ui/icons/lenses ──
Soil data, plant data, moisture, nitrogen, growth, energy, guild, design, normal/overworld.

── ui/icons/hud ──
Tile inspector, forecast/events, ecology scanner (minimap), today's plan (queue), farm hands,
sleep/end turn, almanac, pause, save/load, edge-tab strip icons (replace emoji placeholders).

── ui/icons/build ──
Thumbnails for data_objects.gd + campaign fixtures: house, pig_house, duck_house, pen, gate,
honesty_box, compost_brewer, solar_panel, battery, water_butt, beehive, polytunnel zone.

── ui/icons/items ──
Seed packets, harvest yields, inventory icons, honesty-box produce, upgrade/meta icons.

── ui/cursors ──
Per-tool mouse cursors (32×32): hoe, water, plant, build, demolish, inspect.

── flora/{canopy,understory,groundcover} ──
Default open-core plant sprites (128×128). Custom plants go to user://databases/sprites/.

── flora/seeds ──
Seed-picker icons (small, ~32–48px) per plant id.

── structures/community ──
Honesty box, compost brewer, gate, shared farm fixtures.

── structures/animal_systems ──

── structures/water ──
Water butt, moisture net, sprinkler, swale/rain catchment props.

── structures/automata ──
Drone hub, drone pollinator, smart shade (GVCS / automata campaign).

Polytunnel plastic overlay, path segments, grey-path tiles.

ducks/ (have art), pigs/, bees/ (beehive), generic poultry.
